Starting Off

  • Press 'New Game' to start the game.
  • The game can be played with 2 contestents. Enter their names when prompted to do so.
  • The game begins. Follow along as the gameplay states.
  • The scores will be updated once a game ends.
  • Press 'New Game' to play again. You can choose to follow along with the same or choose new contestents and scores when prompted at the start of a new game.



Gameplay

  • The game follows classic CONNECT 4 rules.
    • Players take alternative turns to drop discs into the grid.
    • The player who manages to place 4 consecutively placed discs (horizontally, vertically or diagonally) first wins.
  • Players may click on any dot in the vertical column they wish to drop their disc in. The disc ends up in the lowest unoccupied slot in the column.
  • The game ends once a player manages a victory sequence or once no more moves are possible.
